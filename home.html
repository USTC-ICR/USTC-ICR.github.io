The Intelligent Control and Robotics (ICR) group at the University of Science and Technology of China focuses on the design and analysis of control algorithms for robotic systems to realize intelligence, efficiency, and safety in uncertain and dynamic environments, which integrate advanced control theory with the science of networks, robotics, and formal methods. Strong emphasis is placed on both theoretic research and experimentation for addressing fundamental and open real-world technological problems. Our aim is to make life better by significantly advancing the technologies on controls and training science-based engineers and professionals to shape the future of our society.
